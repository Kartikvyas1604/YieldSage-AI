# CredChain AI — Hackathon Pitch Deck

## 🎯 The Problem

### DeFi is Broken for 99% of Users

Every lending protocol today requires **$150 collateral to borrow $100**. This locks up **$50 TRILLION** in unnecessary capital and kills adoption.

**Why?** Because there is ZERO credit history on-chain.

- Traditional credit bureaus (FICO, Experian) don't track crypto
- Lending protocols treat everyone as high-risk
- Good borrowers subsidize bad actors' defaults
- No reputation = no trust = no capital efficiency

---

## 💡 The Solution

**CredChain AI** is the world's first AI-powered on-chain credit score for Solana.

We read your complete wallet history and use Claude AI to generate a trustless, verifiable credit score (0-850) that proves your creditworthiness.

### How It Works

1. **Connect Wallet** → User connects Phantom wallet
2. **AI Analysis** → Claude reads full on-chain history via tools
3. **Live Scoring** → 20 signals analyzed across 5 categories
4. **NFT Credential** → Soulbound, non-transferable reputation badge
5. **Protocol Access** → Lending protocols query your score for better rates

2. **Premium Tiers** ($CRED subscriptions)
   - Starter: 50 $CRED/month (3 analyses)
   - Pro: 200 $CRED/month (unlimited analyses)
   - Elite: 500 $CRED/month (API access + alerts)

3. **B2B Licensing**
   - White-label credit scoring for large protocols
